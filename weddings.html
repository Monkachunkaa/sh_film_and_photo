What to Expect

1

Getting Acquainted

After you reach out through our contact form or email, we'll schedule a relaxed phone call or video chat. We'll listen to your vision, answer your questions, and make sure we're the perfect match for your special day. This conversation is all about understanding what matters most to you.

2

Making It Official

Once we connect and you're ready to move forward, we'll send a simple electronic contract for you both to review and sign. A deposit secures your date in our calendar, ensuring we'll be there to capture your celebration. That's it—you're booked!

3

Breaking the Ice

We'll schedule your engagement or bridal session, giving us the perfect opportunity to work together before the wedding day. This relaxed photo session helps everyone feel comfortable in front of the camera and allows us to understand how to best capture your unique connection.

4

Your Wedding Day

On the day you've been dreaming of, our team will be there capturing every meaningful moment—from quiet, emotional instances to joyful celebrations. We blend into the background when needed and guide when helpful, ensuring you can fully immerse yourself in every moment while we preserve these memories for a lifetime.
